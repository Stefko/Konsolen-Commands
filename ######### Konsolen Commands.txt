Komplette Site per SSH packen:
-------------------------------------------------------------------------------------
tar cvfz sitename_$(date "+%Y-%m-%d_%H-%M-%S").tar.gz Ordner/




Site per SSH entpacken:
-------------------------------------------------------------------------------------
tar -xvzf sitename.tar.gz



mySQL Dump erstellen:
-------------------------------------------------------------------------------------
mysqldump -hDATENBANKHOST -uDBUSER -pDBPASSWORT --opt DBNAME | gzip > Website2015/SITENAME.sql.gz



mySQL Dump zurückspielen:
-------------------------------------------------------------------------------------
mysql -hDATENBANKHOST -uDBUSER -pDBPASSWORT DBNAME < dump.sql.gz
